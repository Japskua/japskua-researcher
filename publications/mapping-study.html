Where is the research on connecting game ​ worlds?—A systematic mapping study

In this publication, a systematic mapping study on connecting video games was carried out. The goal of this research was to map the current state-of-the art in the field of connecting video games. The interest of this study was on the academic side, to find out what the is said in the academic literature and do some technical solutions already exist for connecting games together.

The literature search resulted in 23 accepted articles, containing both technology presentations and scientific studies on the possibility of merging multiple virtual worlds together. The study also revealed, that only little research has been done on the field of connecting games and it varies a lot. Semantic technologies, such as ontologies are seen as promising possibility to connect virtual worlds together, although no general solution seems to be done on the matter. The results also pointed out, that researchers do not have clear common concepts or vocabulary for connecting virtual worlds, thus suggesting that the field is still in its infancy.
